The Congress Party

As a 130+ year old institution with a large voter base the Congress party still stands tall on its feet even today. Congress has a strong kundali of its own, so to speak, but it goes without saying that the future of the party is intertwined with that of its leaders.

The 7th house denotes partnerships, which will form the centerpiece of politics from Priyanka Gandhi. This is also what she did with the Samajwadi Party in the last UP elections. While she will help Congress gain some lost ground her long term role in the party is not going to be very fruitful.
