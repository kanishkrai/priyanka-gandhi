Synopsis

There is a general assumption that Ms. Priyanka Gandhi is a replica of our late primeminister Shrimati Indira Gandhi but this is not true at all. From an astrological standpoint, it is not in Priyanka's stars to be able to cut a prodigious career in politics. She will not be able to save the Indian National Congress from sinking under the aegis of Mr. Rahul Gandhi.

So the question is: Will Ms. Priyanka assume responsibility and take over the reins of Congress? A look at the natal chart of Priyanka suggests that yes she will do so but what else is in store for her in politics?

Priyanka Gandhi is born under the expressive Gemini Ascendant and secretive Scorpio Moon sign. Her Lagna Lord Mercury is conjoined with the 10th lord Jupiter along with Sun denoting a political career but only a conflicted one. Planet Mars in the 10th house indicates she has great command over people.
